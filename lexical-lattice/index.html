Lexical Lattice

Daily word puzzle with hint analytics. Can you solve it in one guess?

Lexical Lattice is a daily word puzzle game with a strategic twist — spend tokens to unlock powerful hint analytics that show you every possible remaining word.

One word. Five attempts. Every day.

Like the word puzzles you already love, but smarter. Green means the letter is in the right spot. Yellow means it's in the word but wrong position. Gray means it's not in the word at all.

Hint analytics that change how you think

Before you spend tokens, you always see the total count of possible words remaining. Decide whether a hint is worth it. Spend 5 tokens to reveal the full list — then tap any word to automatically fill it in.

Earn tokens just by playing

Solve in 1–2 guesses: +3 tokens. Solve in 3: +2 tokens. Solve in 4–5: +1 token. No ads required to earn. No pay-to-win — hints are a quality-of-life tool, not a shortcut to victory.

The Perfect 1.0 achievement

Solve the word in a single guess and trigger the full celebration: confetti, neon effects, and your solve is automatically saved to your personal Hall of Fame. Share it with friends.

Your stats, your progress

Track your win streak, best streak, win rate, average guesses, and your collection of one-guess solves. Everything stored locally on your device — no account required.
