Scry

MTG Arena limited deck evaluator for Android.

Scry is a card evaluation tool for Magic: The Gathering Arena limited formats. Browse every Arena set, analyze your drafted deck, simulate opening hands, and look up 17lands win-rate grades — all in one fast, offline-capable app.

Card Browser

Browse all Arena sets with full card images and 17lands ratings. Filter by set, search by name, and tap any card for a detailed view showing mana cost, oracle text, rarity, and its GIH win rate grade (A+ through F).

Deck Analyzer

Paste an MTGA deck export and instantly see your mana curve, color distribution, per-card ratings, and a land count recommendation based on your curve.

Mulligan Simulator

Draw simulated opening hands from your imported deck with real card images. Re-draw as many times as you like to get a feel for your deck's consistency before you play.

Always Up To Date

On launch, Scry automatically checks for newly released Arena sets and downloads any missing card data and images. No manual updates needed.
